Hello fungibility

Goodbye discrimination.

Anonymous

OZCoin separates knowing what you have from how you got it.  The cryptogrpahy we use ensures that transactions cannot be linked to the receiver’s address.  This allows you to spend more time worrying about how to spend your money, and less about where it came from.

Confidential

The amount enclosed in every transaction is confidential.  Only the sender and receiver can learn the amount that was sent, but anyone can verify that the amount is valid.  Since these input and output amounts are no longer linkable, we can achieve stronger anonymity guarantees.

Fungible

By combining anonymity and confidentiality, OZCoins behave much like cash.  Users are statistically unaware whether or not the received amount is tainted.  As such, they can be spent and transacted in a way that resists discrimination from merchants and institutions.

What would it take to create a fungible cryptocurrency?

The idea for OZCoin was originally conceived at MIT for the CryptoHacks hackathon. Our initial inspiration: what would it require to create a fungible cryptocurrency?.

We leverage blockchain technology and cutting-edge cryptography to provide users with practical fungibility, while enabling full anonymity and confidentiality out of necessity.  OZRS, short for "One-time Zero-sum Ring Signatures" is a new approach to transactional mechanics.  OZRS permits transactions to occur, while divulging the minimal information about a coin’s history.  Ignorance is bliss–and so is using OZCoin.
